## Key Findings

**Pilot scope:** Safety bicycle, 1876–1892, 4 commits, 15 sources, 1 subcomponent.

**Core transition:** C→D (Rover Safety → Pneumatic-tire Safety, 1888). Chosen because:
- Subjective pressures are clearly evidenced (advertising, press commentary, cultural study)
- Hybrid annotation meaningfully improves explanation of the 1890s cycling boom
- Objective-only account would miss the demographic/social expansion

**Outcome:** Outcome B (hybrid slightly better overall). Not every transition shows dramatic hybrid value, but at least one does — sufficient to justify the framework extension.

**Confidence discipline:** Strong for artifact specs and Dunlop tire benefit; medium for marketing/perception effects; low for fashion/status signaling.

## What This Proves

A small, source-backed, version-controlled material artifact lineage can be constructed with:
- Discrete, meaningful commits
- Clear objective/subjective pressure separation
- Complete provenance (every claim traceable)
- Plausible, non-speculative subjective annotations
- Shallow subcomponent recursion without scope explosion

The dataset is small enough to be manually verified but structured enough to scale.
